Project Description

  • Financials Dashboard
    The Financials Dashboard is a comprehensive tool designed to provide a visual representation of key financial metrics and performance indicators for a business or organization. It includes real-time updates on revenue, expenses, profit margins, and other relevant financial data, allowing stakeholders to make informed decisions based on the current financial health of the entity.
  • Sales Dashboard
    The Sales Dashboard offers a dynamic overview of sales performance, tracking important metrics such as total sales, product-wise sales, customer demographics, and sales trends over time. This tool facilitates data-driven decision-making by providing a clear visualization of the sales pipeline, allowing businesses to identify opportunities, optimize strategies, and enhance overall sales efficiency.
  • IMDB Analysis Report
    The IMDB Analysis Report delves into the world of movies by exploring data from the Internet Movie Database (IMDB). This report includes insights into movie ratings, genres, actor popularity, and other relevant factors, enabling a deeper understanding of trends and patterns within the film industry.
  • Complete Step for GBMs Regression (Cross Equatorial Northerly Surge - CENS Dataset)
    This summary outlines a comprehensive step-by-step process for implementing Gradient Boosted Machines (GBMs) including XGBoost, LightGBM and CatBoost for Regression on the Cross Equatorial Northerly Surge (CENS) dataset. In this project, I utilized feature selection techniques from SHAP and Catboost, complemented by fine-tuning using the Optuna module. The objective is to predict and analyze the occurrence of CENS phenomena, offering valuable insights for meteorologists and researchers studying tropical weather patterns.
  • Comparing CONV1D, LSTM, and Hybrid Model for Rainfall Forecasting
    This project involves a comparative analysis of Convolutional Neural Networks (CONV1D), Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks, and CONV1D-LSTM for rainfall forecasting. By evaluating the performance of these models, the project aims to identify the most effective approach for accurate and reliable rainfall predictions, crucial for various applications in meteorology and water resource management.
  • Correlation Map for Spatial Datasets
    Focusing on spatial datasets, this project generates a correlation map to visualize relationships and patterns between different spatial variables. By mapping correlations, it provides valuable insights into how different factors interact within a spatial context, aiding in better understanding and decision-making for fields such as geography, environmental science, and urban planning.
  • Quick Generate Atmospheric Datasets (ERA5)
    Focused on efficiency, this summary outlines a streamlined process for rapidly generating atmospheric datasets using the ERA5 dataset. By providing quick access to atmospheric data, it facilitates research and analysis in meteorology, climate science, and related fields.
  • GBMs Classification for CENS Phenomenon
    Building on the CENS dataset, this summary employs Gradient Boosted Machines (GBMs) for classification tasks related to the Cross Equatorial Northerly Surge (CENS) phenomenon. The classification model aims to accurately categorize instances of CENS, contributing to a better understanding of this meteorological phenomenon.
  • Credit Card Fraud Classification and Comparing ML Performance
    Focusing on cybersecurity, this summary involves the development and comparison of machine learning models for credit card fraud detection. By evaluating the performance of different algorithms, it provides insights into the effectiveness of various approaches in identifying and preventing fraudulent transactions.
  • Restaurant Bankruptcy Analysis
    This summary conducts a detailed analysis of factors contributing to restaurant bankruptcies. By examining financial and operational data, it aims to identify key indicators and patterns associated with business failures in the restaurant industry, providing stakeholders with valuable insights for risk management and decision-making. In this project, I encountered an extremely imbalanced class issue and a substantial amount of missing data. To address these challenges, I attempted to mitigate the problems by implementing KNN Imputer and various resampling techniques.
  • Top Rated Movie Analysis
    The Top Rated Movie Analysis summary explores and analyzes factors contributing to the high ratings of movies. By considering variables such as genre, director, and cast, it aims to uncover patterns that influence movie ratings, offering valuable insights for filmmakers, studios, and enthusiasts interested in understanding the elements that contribute to cinematic success.
